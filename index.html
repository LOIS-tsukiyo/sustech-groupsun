June,2026Xiamen University

Academician Sun Xiaowei visits Academician Hu Wenping, President of Xiamen University

Recently, Sun Xiaowei, a foreign academician of the Russian Academy of Sciences, the executive dean of the Institute of Nanoscience and Application of the SUST, and the chair professor of the Department of EEE, visited Hu Wenping, an academician of the CAS Member and the president of Xiamen University. The two sides carried out relaxed and friendly academic exchanges around discipline construction, scientific research cooperation, talent cultivation, achievement transformation, optoelectronics and nano frontier development.

May,2026Nature

Academician Sun Xiaowei's team was invited to publish a research review article in Nature

Recently, at the invitation of the top academic journal Nature, Sun Xiaowei, Chairman of MiLEDA, Foreign Academician of the Russian Academy of Sciences, Executive Dean of the Institute of Nanoscience and Applications at SUST, and Chair Professor of the Department of EEE, published a research review article titled "Glasses free display switches between 2D and 3D" in the Nature News&Views column, commenting on the latest progress made by the joint team of POSTECH in the field of switchable naked eye 2D/3D displays.
